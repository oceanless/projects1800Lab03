Frequently Asked Questions



I forgot the password to my account, what do I do now?

Once you enter your mail you will find a "Trouble signing in?" button below, click that and a mail will be sent to your registered ID with instructions to reset your password.

Is my information on the app kept private?

The Two Do's app does not collect personal information, with all the information you provide stored locally on your device. You may want to consider selecting a secure password to keep your information private.

What should I do if I am feeling distressed while using the app?

If you are feeling distressed and think you may act on thoughts to hurt yourself and need help, you can access a crisis line near you:

Call Canadian Suicide Prevention Support Line: 1-833-456-4566

Link to Crisis Centers in Canada: crisisservicescanada.ca

You could also call 911, or go to your local hospital emergency room
